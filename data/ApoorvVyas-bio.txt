Apoorv is currently a third-year Ph.D. student at EPFL and Idiap research
institute, where he is co-supervised by Prof. Hervé Bourlard  and Prof.
François Fleuret. He is currently working on the application of deep learning
to improve the performance of automatic speech recognition (ASR) models with
limited supervised data.

Prior to joining Idiap, Apoorv received a degree in Bachelor of Technology in
Electronics & Electrical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology,
Guwahati, in 2014. After this, he worked as a Systems Engineer at Intel Labs,
India. He is a co-inventor on 5 patents and has published at tier-one
conferences such as ECCV, ICML, Neurips, ICASSP, and Interspeech.
